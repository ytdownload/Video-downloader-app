How It Works

RapidGrab does not host any content. When you submit a URL, this website sends a request to our backend server. The server then communicates directly with YouTube's services to get video information and stream data. We then present these direct download links to you. Because we rely on direct communication, our service can be affected by network conditions or changes to YouTube's platform. We believe in providing a simple interface for personal, fair-use purposes, such as offline viewing or creating backups of your own content.
